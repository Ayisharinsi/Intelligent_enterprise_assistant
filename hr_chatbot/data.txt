Company Overview:
Our organization is a leading public sector enterprise committed to innovation, employee welfare, and social responsibility. The company promotes a culture of teamwork, continuous learning, and integrity.

Company Working Hours:
Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Saturday and Sunday are official holidays.

Leave Policies:
- Casual Leave: 12 days per year, can be used for personal or urgent matters.
- Sick Leave: Up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year with a valid medical certificate.
- Earned Leave: Employees can earn 1.5 days per completed month of service.
- Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

Payroll and HR:
For payroll or salary issues, email payroll@organization.com.
For HR-related queries, email hr@organization.com.

Experience Certificate:
To request an experience certificate, send an email to hr@organization.com mentioning your employee ID and last working day.

IT Support:
- For password reset, visit password.organization.com/reset.
- For slow system performance, restart and clear temporary files. If the issue persists, contact IT support via helpdesk@organization.com.
- For software installation, employees must raise an IT service request.

Events and Training:
The company organizes monthly team-building, awareness, and technical training sessions. Announcements are shared through the internal portal and company email.

Dress Code and Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professional attire during office hours. Offensive language or behavior is not permitted.

Work from Home Policy:
Work-from-home may be approved by department heads under special circumstances.

Employee Portal:
All employees can access HR, IT, and payroll services through the internal portal at portal.organization.com.
